In most daos governance, a single proposal usually starts from forum discussion, moving to an offchain temperature check, usually in Snapshot, before moving onchain to Tally or other onchain voting tool. All these steps happen across different tools and different moments in time

That makes it hard to answer simple questions like: what stage is this proposal in right now? Is it executed yet? All because the information is very fragmented.

The Governance Tracker brings that lifecycle together, so you can quickly see whether a proposal is still in discussion, already voted offchain, moving onchain, or fully executed.

Instead of jumping across governance tools, you can search once, scan the current stage, and jump directly to the relevant source.


You can look for an specific proposal, or filter by the status of the different stages, and see exactly at which point of the lifecycle is each proposal.
